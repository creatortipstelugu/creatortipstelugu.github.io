Core Capabilities

Nano Banana is more than a text-to-image generator. It's an integrated suite of visual tools powered by conversation. Explore its key features below by clicking on the tabs.

Prompt-Based Conversational Editing

This is the model's standout feature. Users can upload an image and provide simple, natural language instructions to make precise changes. Instead of complex masking or selection tools, you can just ask. This enables an iterative workflow where you refine an image through dialogue.

Example Prompt: "Take this photo of a person on a beach and remove the other people from the background."

Follow-up: "Great. Now make the sky look like it's sunset."

Character & Style Consistency

A major challenge in AI image generation has been maintaining the identity of a subject across multiple images. Nano Banana excels at this, allowing creators to place the same character in different scenes, showcase a product from a variety of angles, or generate consistent brand assets, all while preserving the subject‚Äôs key features.

Example Prompt: "Generate an image of a cartoon robot character. Now, show the same robot sitting in a park."

Multi-Image Fusion

The model can understand and merge multiple input images into a single, cohesive new visual. This is powerful for compositing, such as placing a product into a new lifestyle scene, restyling a room with a texture from another image, or combining elements from different photos into a new artistic creation.

Example Use: Upload a photo of a sofa and a photo of an empty living room. Prompt: "Place this sofa in the living room against the far wall."

Visual Reasoning & World Knowledge

Leveraging Gemini's broader world knowledge, the model can perform tasks that require a true understanding of what it sees. It's not just matching pixels; it's comprehending concepts. This unlocks use cases like solving a hand-drawn math equation, generating an accurate diagram from a description, or following complex editing instructions that rely on real-world logic.

Example Use: Upload a diagram of the water cycle with a missing label. Prompt: "What is the name of the process for the arrow pointing from the ocean to the clouds?"
